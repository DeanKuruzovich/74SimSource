74 Series Logic Chips

The 74 series is a family of transistor to transistor logic (TTL) and CMOS integrated circuits that have been an industry standard since the 1960s. Each part number encodes the chip's logic function, making it easy to find a component that does exactly what you need.

Part Number Format

A typical part number looks like 74HC00:

  • 74 identifies the part as a member of the 74 series family.
  • HC the logic subfamily (see table below).
  • 00 the function code (quad 2 input NAND gates in this case).

Logic Subfamilies

Subfamily Full name Notes
LS Low power Schottky TTL Classic TTL replacement; 5 V supply, moderate speed
HC High speed CMOS Wide supply range (2 to 6 V), low power, CMOS level inputs
HCT High speed CMOS, TTL compatible inputs Same as HC but accepts TTL logic levels; easy LS drop in replacement
AHC Advanced high speed CMOS Faster than HC with lower output impedance
LV Low voltage CMOS 3.3 V operation
